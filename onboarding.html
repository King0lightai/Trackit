Welcome to Titus Project Tracker!

Thank you for installing our extension. Titus Project Tracker helps you organize product selections, track lead times, and manage deliveries for all your remodeling projects. Save any product while browsing with just a few clicks!

Key Features

Save Products from Any Website

Click the extension icon or right-click on any product image to save it to your projects.

Organize by Projects

Create and manage multiple projects, keeping all your selections organized in one place.

Track Lead Times & Deliveries

Never miss a delivery with our timeline view and delivery reminders.

Share with Clients & Team

Export your projects to share with clients and team members easily.

Getting Started

1

Create Your First Project

Start by creating a new project from the dashboard. Give it a name and description.

2

Browse & Save Products

When you find a product you like on any website, click the extension icon to save it to your project.

3

Add Details & Organize

Add lead times, expected delivery dates, and organize your products by category.

4

Track & Share

Use the timeline view to track deliveries and export your project to share with others.
